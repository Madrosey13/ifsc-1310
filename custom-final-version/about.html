What is the M.M.A.C?

The Missing Media Archival Commitee, or M.M.A.C. for short, is a group of multi media archivists that look to find lost media. We go through news reports, VHS tapes, and other forms of media to find pieces of lost media. Founded in late April in 2023, we are a fairly recent group. While we handled smaller pieces of lost media, this is our first entry into a larger scale project.and it hurts

Our plan is to keep this site updated monthly to keep onlookers updated.
